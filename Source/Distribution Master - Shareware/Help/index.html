3D Mail Effects (a.k.a. 3DME) is a program that notifies you when you receive new Email in a variety of ways (3D rotating objects, sounds, blinking keyboard lights, etc.)

This documentation will help you setup & configure 3DME for your email accounts and help you understand how to use its features.

If you are running 3DME for the first time, click here.
