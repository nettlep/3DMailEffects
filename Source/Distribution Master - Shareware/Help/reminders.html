The purpose of the Reminder preferences is to allow you to define what will happen when a reminder is triggered. Reminders are triggered when new mail is present, and the specified time interval (see Play every ??? seconds below) elapses for an account. You can choose to do nothing, or you can cause 3DME to notify you in some fashion.

A very common example of a reminder, is to play a sound every 15 minutes (900 seconds) to remind you that new mail is pending.

      Play a reminder sound -- Put simply, play a sound with the reminder.

      WAV file -- If Sound an alarm when new mail arrives is selected, you may enter a sound file here. Click on the "..." button to browse for a WAV file, and click on the speaker icon to test the sound that you've chosen.

      Play every ??? seconds -- Specify the elapsed time (in seconds) between reminders.

      Run a program with this reminder -- This allows you to setup Runs to execute with new mail. Runs are nothing more than a small batch of programs (or just one program) to execute.
